INDIVIDUAL CREDIT SCORING PIPELINE — MonarchCredit

PURPOSE
Scores individual borrowers (not businesses) for financial distress / default risk.
Targets under-banked and unbanked individuals who lack traditional credit history.
Uses 61 engineered SME features derived from alternative data sources.

61 ENGINEERED FEATURES (SME_ prefix)
Financial metrics:
- SME_Revenue_Volatility_Index: variability in business revenue
- SME_Working_Capital_Adequacy_Ratio: working capital vs liabilities
- SME_Cash_Conversion_Efficiency: how efficiently cash is generated from operations
- SME_Operating_Expense_Ratio: expenses as proportion of revenue
- SME_Working_Capital_Cycle_Days: days to convert working capital
- SME_Capital_Efficiency_Ratio: output per unit of capital invested
- SME_Capital_Retention_Ratio: retained earnings ratio
- SME_Gross_Profit_Proxy: estimated gross profit margin

Risk and stress indicators:
- SME_Business_Runway_Months: estimated months of operational runway
- SME_Sector_Resilience_Rating: sector-level risk resilience score
- SME_Sector_Monsoon_Stress: exposure to monsoon/seasonal stress (India-specific)
- SME_Sector_Volatility_Stress: sector volatility exposure
- SME_Sector_Economic_Cycle_Resilience: resilience to economic cycles
- SME_Sector_Default_Density_Score: density of defaults in same sector
- Liquidity_Risk_Index: composite liquidity risk
- Financial_Pressure_Index: composite financial pressure
- Credit_Stress_Index: credit-specific stress indicator
- Repayment_Risk_Amplifier: risk amplifier based on repayment patterns

Growth and market indicators:
- SME_Income_Growth_Momentum: revenue growth trend
- SME_Market_Expansion_Score: market expansion activity
- SME_Business_Scalability_Index: scalability potential
- SME_Technology_Adoption_Proxy: technology usage indicator
- SME_Product_Diversification_Score: product line diversity
- SME_Growth_Stability_Score: growth consistency

Network and social indicators:
- SME_Business_Network_Strength_Score: business relationship network
- SME_Business_Social_Network_Risk: social network risk signals
- SME_Regional_Business_Ecosystem_Quality: local business environment
- SME_Industry_Competition_Pressure_Index: competitive pressure

Credit behavior:
- SME_Credit_Bureau_Inquiry_Velocity: frequency of credit inquiries
- SME_Credit_Utilization_Intensity: credit utilization level
- SME_Repayment_Plan_Instability: inconsistency in repayment
- SME_Inquiry_Clustering_Score: clustering of multiple inquiries
- SME_Business_Leverage_Spiral_Flag: flag for dangerous leverage trends

Stability and documentation:
- SME_Business_Longevity_Score: business age and track record
- SME_Address_Stability_Business_Flag: address stability indicator
- SME_Business_Documentation_Completeness: completeness of business docs
- SME_Business_Asset_Backing_Ratio: assets backing the business
- SME_Business_Skin_In_The_Game: owner investment commitment

Composite scores (engineered):
- Growth_Stability_Score: combined growth and stability
- Operational_Resilience_Index: operational resilience composite
- Asset_Support_Strength: asset-backed support strength
- Cash_Conversion_Stability: stability of cash conversion
- Credit_Dependency_Index: reliance on credit
- Business_Stability_Score: overall business stability

MODEL ARCHITECTURE — Stacking Ensemble
Base models (Layer 1):
1. Random Forest: 100-500 estimators, max_depth 4-12, class_weight=balanced_subsample
2. XGBoost: 200-800 estimators, max_depth 3-7, learning_rate 0.005-0.10
3. Logistic Regression: C range 0.0001-10, penalties l1/l2/elasticnet, solver=saga

Meta-learner (Layer 2):
- LogisticRegression with C=0.05 (tighter regularization)
- passthrough=True — meta-learner sees raw features + base model probabilities
- 5-fold OOF cross-validation for meta-feature generation

Post-processing:
- Isotonic calibration on 15% held-out split (maps scores to true default rates)
- Threshold optimization on separate validation split (not training data)
- Threshold metric: F1 (or precision with min_recall=0.60 constraint)

HYPERPARAMETER TUNING (Optuna)
- Random Forest: 20 trials, TPE sampler, MedianPruner
- XGBoost: 50 trials (most complex search space)
- Logistic Regression: 15 trials
- Feature selection before tuning: variance_threshold=0.01, correlation_threshold=0.95
- Sample 80,000 rows for tuning (from fit set only, after calibration split)

DATABASE TABLES
credit_warehouse.duckdb contains:
- individual_training_data: main training table (TARGET column)
- individual_test_data: main test table
- indian_train_data: Indian screener dataset train split (292 rows, 60 features)
- indian_test_data: Indian screener dataset test split (147 rows, 60 features)
- indian_full_processed: full Indian dataset (439 companies, 10 years)
- msme_training_data: MSME data with 61 SME features (TARGET column)
- msme_test_data: MSME test data with 61 SME features

INDIAN DATASET (dual-stream mode)
- 439 company-year observations from Indian screener
- 10 years of data: 2016-2025
- 50 companies tracked
- Target: Financial_Distress (1=distressed, 0=healthy)
- Default rate: ~10% overall
- Most distressed companies: Palash_Securities (60%), Reganto_Enterprises (60%)
- Features: 60 financial ratio features
